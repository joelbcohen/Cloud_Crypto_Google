Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

Your privacy is our priority. We do not collect or store any personal information.

Overview

At Cloud Crypto, we believe in transparency and protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains our approach to data collection and usage.

Data Collection

We do not collect or store any personal information. Our platform is designed to operate without requiring or retaining any personally identifiable information about our users.

This means we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data or IP addresses
  • Device identifiers beyond what's necessary for watch registration
  • Browsing history or usage patterns
  • Any other personal or sensitive information

Device Registration

Watch devices registered on our network use secure attestation methods to verify authenticity. Device serial numbers are used solely for ensuring one device per user and preventing duplicate registrations. These identifiers are cryptographically secured and cannot be linked back to individual users.

Network Statistics

The network statistics displayed on our website (such as total accounts, transactions, and device counts) are aggregated, anonymous data that cannot be traced back to individual users or devices. This information is used solely to provide transparency about network activity.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website does not use cookies or any tracking technologies to monitor your activity. We do not use analytics services, advertising networks, or any third-party tracking tools.

Third-Party Services

We do not share any information with third parties because we do not collect any information to share. Our platform operates independently without reliance on external data processors or service providers that would require access to user data.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store personal information, there is no personal data for you to access, modify, or delete. You can use our platform with confidence knowing that your privacy is inherently protected by our design.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our practices, please feel free to reach out to us through the contact information provided on our main website.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
